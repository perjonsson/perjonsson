about me

I combine over a decade of experience from the corporate world (ABB, OC Oerlikon), the non-profit sector (Young Entrepreneurs of Sweden) and fast-growing internet startups (Omnicloud, Jumpstarter) into my work as speaker, facilitator and co-founder of Everyday School.

My mission is to create physical spaces where people can be their authentic selves and learn things together.
