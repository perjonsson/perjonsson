Mission

When I was ten years old someone told me that I was great at computers. They quickly became my life and I used them in everything from play to work.

After over a decade of hiding behind of a computer screen I had finally discovered my life's mission: to create platforms where meaningful conversations happen. Conversations about love, relationships, work, meaning and all the big themes in life. The ones we want to have but have a hard time making room for.

Today I am all about finding ways of realizing that mission. I engage in businesses that are driven by cause and purpose, with people who are committed to creating meaningful results.
