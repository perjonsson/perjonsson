Mission

I began my corporate career at 18 as a sales manager at ABB, managing multimillion dollar power transmission deals towards governments in the Middle East and Africa.

I began my startup career at 19, founding Young Entrepreneurs of Sweden, a network for the foremost young entrepreneurs in Sweden.

I started playing with computers when I was ten and had my first client when I was 15. When I was 23 I founded Omnicloud, a software development company, and in 2012 I founded Jumpstarter, an application hosting and development platform. By 2014 I had raised $2M in funding and hired a staff of ten.

Todat I run Everyday, the school for everything you didn't learn in school, where you develop and grow in subjects like communication, love, relationships, work, meaning and health.

I'm passionate about creating those conversations that we never make room for in our fast-paced lives.
