Mission

I have been dealing with computers since I was ten. Someone much older than me told me that I was great with them and that was enough for me to create a life where computers became my primary way to relate to the world. I used them to find friends, play games, write code, design websites and build entire companies.

Over a decade later I found an even deeper mission: to create and nurture meaningful conversation. The conversations that challenge us to grow as individuals. The ones we want to have but have a hard time making room for in our fast-paced lives.

Today my work starts with a single question: how can we make people thrive and not just survive? I engage in causes who turn that question into action. Causes backed by passionate people who have the courage to ask what no one else does. If that is you, let's talk.
